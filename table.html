Here is how to be respectful on the mountain:









This is what you Should do This is What not to do Why?
Give the person to your sides some space. Ride close to people If you ride to close to somewhere you are more likely to accidently hit them
Hit the large jumps when you first begin Hit every feature when you're still learning how to ride The terrain park is for advanced riders and you can be easily injured if you're new to snowboarding
Give person in front of you room in the chairlift line Hit your skis or snowboard on the persons equipment in front of you in the chairlift line I have seen people become very mad when their equipment is hit. It would be respectful to give room to fellow riders
